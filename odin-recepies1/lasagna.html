Lasagna Recipe

Enjoy this classic lasagna recipe!

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that you can try at home!

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna noodles
  2. Ground beef
  3. Tomato sauce
  4. Ricotta cheese
  5. Shredded mozzarella cheese
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Garlic
  8. Onion
  9. Olive oil
  10. Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!

Tips

  • For extra flavor, add herbs like basil or oregano to the meat sauce.
  • Let the lasagna sit for 10-15 minutes before slicing to help it hold its shape.
  • You can use no-boil lasagna noodles to save time.
  • Feel free to add vegetables like spinach or mushrooms for added nutrition.

Frequently Asked Questions

1.Can I make lasagna ahead of time?

Yes, you can prepare the lasagna in advance and refrigerate it. Just cover it tightly with foil and bake it when you're ready to serve.

2.Can I freeze lasagna?

Absolutely! You can freeze lasagna before or after baking. Just make sure to wrap it well in plastic wrap and aluminum foil. When ready to eat, thaw it in the refrigerator overnight and then bake as usual.

3.How long does lasagna last in the fridge?

Cooked lasagna can be stored in the refrigerator for about 3-5 days. Make sure to keep it in an airtight container.

4.Can I use different types of cheese?

Yes, you can use different cheeses like provolone, gouda, or even vegan cheese alternatives to suit your taste.
